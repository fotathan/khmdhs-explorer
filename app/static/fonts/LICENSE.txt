Fira Sans Condensed and Fira Mono — self-hosted webfont subsets
================================================================

These .woff2 files (latin, latin-ext, greek subsets) and fira.css are vendored
from Google Fonts so this app serves its own webfonts and pulls no third-party
frontend resources.
